Red scarf, pink scarf,
Tied in a bow.
You're my pretty
Don't you know?

Cool black cat
With pupils small,
Eyes of caramel,
Ears stand tall.

Caramel cat
With eyes of blue.
Stripes run softly,
Two by two.

No pale dishrag
Tied in a knot.
I'm you're pretty,
You forgot.
